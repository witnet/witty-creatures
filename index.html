HOW TO PLAY
  1. This is an off-chain trading card game — the precursors of NFTs — organized by Witnet, the decentralized oracle network, exclusively for attendees of EthCC[4].
  2. There are 12 different models of premium, gold plated PCB cards in the game.
  3. Each card has a different #WittyCreature on its front side.
  4. Each card has 3 secret words written in fine print on the bottom of its front side.
  5. These secret words are part of one or more backup secret phrases (BIP-39 mnemonics).
  6. These backup phrases unlock Ethereum accounts with generous prizes deposited on them.
  7. These words can also be loaded into the Sheikah Witnet Wallet app to receive additional prizes in Wit tokens .
  8. There are 3 bronze prizes (0.25 ETH each), 2 silver prizes (0.5 ETH each) and 1 gold prize (1ETH).
  9. If you want to discover all the 36 secret words in the game, you cannot HOLD your cards — you will need to exchange them with other players.
  10. In the Witnet booth at EthCC[4], you can get a leaflet with a word chart in which you can write down the secret words found in each of the cards that you collect.
  11. To reconstruct the backup secret phrases from the individual words and claim the prizes, simply follow the prizes cheatsheet that you will find in the leaflet.
  12. As soon as you have collected the 12 words needed for a prize, make sure to load them into your favorite wallet software and transfer all the available balance to your own Ethereum or Witnet wallet.
  13. You can track which prizes have been taken and which are still standing in the chart above.
